Discography: A Sonic Arsenal



2012 - Kinetik Festival Volume 5 (Various Artists, Artoffact Records): RiotLegion made its global debut with this compilation, introducing its signature ferocity.
2012 - FGFC820 – Homeland Insecurity (Deathwatch Asia): RiotLegion lent its venomous touch to this politically charged album.
2013 - "Legion of Chaos" (Promo Edition): Released as a limited promotional run for DJs and RiotLegion’s live show audience, this early edition captured the raw energy of RiotLegion’s performances.
2014 - Empyrean Asunder – Hate: Expanded & Remastered (Self-released): RiotLegion’s remixes sharpened the edges of this visceral EP.
2013 - "Legion of Chaos" (Higher Circuit Productions): A more polished version of the previously released promotional version, featured "For Justice."
2015 - "GOD(B)LESS": A scathing critique of modern politics, this EP paired brutal electronics with incisive commentary, anchored by tracks like "GOD(B)LESS"
2016 - "Trigger Warning" (Blind Mice Productions): A declaration of war on societal complacency."All I Can Do" delivered infectious grooves, while "Adaptation to Circumstance" pushed the BPM into uncharted territory.
2020 - "Machine Liberation" (Blind Mice Productions): Tracks like "Liberation" and "Jacked In" turned dystopian themes into industrial anthems for the end times.
2022 - Refuse: Volume One (Crunch Pod Records): A peek at tracks that had never been released.
